Working at BitPay

We ask and answer Bitcoin's hardest questions, every day. Our culture values openness, debate, and action.

We're independent.

We're a very flat organization — everyone has ownership, and everyone has the responsibility and resources they need to get the job done.

We're agile.

We're constantly refining and improving our understanding of our customers and our product. We build and ship code quickly, deploying multiple times per day.

We're flexible.

Time with the team in a BitPay office is crucial, but we work effectively out of office, too. Our only mandate? Make a great bitcoin payment experience for our merchants.

We love open source.

Our team is dedicated to producing world-class open source software. We engage with the open source community and welcome feedback and pull requests.

We love bitcoin.

We love the technology that's driving our work. Even out of the office, we find ourselves talking about Bitcoin and its potential.
