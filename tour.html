Why Bitcoin?

Keep more of your money.

Receive 100% of what you charge for every transaction — BitPay doesn't charge processing fees. All pricing plans include unlimited processing volume, conversion, and settlement to your bank account.

Sell to anyone, anywhere.

Connect to the world’s first borderless payment network - Bitcoin. Accept payment in any amount, from anywhere in the world, from any computer or mobile device.

End chargeback fraud and identity theft.

Other payment methods force customers and businesses to shoulder the risks and costs of payment fraud. With bitcoin, customers can pay without handing over sensitive personal information, and refunds are made through the merchant — no chargebacks.

Daily Bank Deposits

BitPay allows you to accept payments in bitcoin and receive exactly what you charge, directly to your bank account, without fees. Bank deposits in 38 countries, settled in US Dollars, Euros, CAD, and more.

Your customer chooses to pay with bitcoin during the checkout process.

They pay the BitPay invoice at a locked-in exchange rate — you get what you charge.

BitPay instantly converts the bitcoin, shielding you from any volatility risk.

Get daily deposits into your bank account without risk or processing fees.
