Getting Started

BitPay’s 40+ bitcoin integrations make accepting bitcoin payments fast and reliable. To get started, sign up for a BitPay account.

Accepting a Bitcoin Payment

With bitcoin, there's no sensitive customer information to collect and store, and there are no cards to charge. Customers simply send bitcoin from their computer or mobile device directly to a bitcoin payment address.

Once received, BitPay converts the bitcoin to your preferred currency and adds the full amount of the payment – without fees – to your next BitPay account settlement. Funds are then deposited to your bank account or bitcoin address.

You can accept bitcoin in a variety of ways. Choose a method below to get started.

Online Payments

Bitcoin makes the online payment experience simple and convenient for customers on any device – customers can pay with just two taps, no sensitive personal information needed.

Shopping Cart Plug-Ins
