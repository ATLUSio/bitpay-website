Selling In Store

BitPay provides brick and mortar merchants with a full array of options for accepting bitcoin payments, from direct point-of-sale integrations to web and mobile-based apps. Get started by signing up for a BitPay account.

Bitcoin Checkout

Bitcoin Checkout is a simple mobile tool for merchants and their staff to accept bitcoin – all with no additional setup.

The app can be downloaded for iOS and Android devices and customized for tipping, order number tracking, and use among multiple staff members.

To accept a payment, simply enter an amount, an order number and gratuity amount (if enabled), and allow your customer to scan the QR code the app generates.

Web Checkout

No app? No problem. Our web-based Quick Checkout does just about everything Bitcoin Checkout does from any web browser on any computer or other device.

Direct Point of Sale Integrations

Get access to BitPay's bitcoin payment processing on full-fledged traditional and tablet-based point of sale systems. Check out all of our direct POS integration options on our Integrations page.
